About Me

Hello. I'm Clare Nash, a registered nurse working in the NHS since 1990 and the founder of the Ecometrics tool. I have a passion for improving healthcare through innovative solutions and believe in the power of data-driven decision-making.

Ecometrics Tool

The Ecometrics tool is designed to provide healthcare professionals with the metrics to choose greener clinical products. My goal is to see adoption and spread of the tool to get the NHS to net zero faster.
